Welcome to Arima Onsen

Experience the timeless tranquility of one of Japan's most renowned hot springs.

Brief History

Arima Onsen, nestled in the mountains of Hyogo Prefecture, is celebrated as one of Japan's oldest hot spring resorts. With over a millennium of history, it has been a retreat for emperors, samurai, and travelers seeking relaxation and healing. The area's unique gold and silver springs have drawn visitors from far and wide, making it a cornerstone of Japanese culture and wellness.

About Arima Onsen

Located in the mountains of Hyogo Prefecture, Arima Onsen is a serene getaway known for its therapeutic waters, breathtaking scenery, and rich history that spans over a millennium.

Why Visit Arima Onsen?

  • Therapeutic Hot Springs: Enjoy the unique "Gold" (kin-sen) and "Silver" (gin-sen) hot springs, each offering distinct health benefits.
  • Traditional Japanese Hospitality: Stay in charming ryokan inns with exceptional service.
  • Scenic Beauty: Surrounded by lush mountains and tranquil nature, Arima Onsen offers a peaceful retreat.
  • Cultural Experiences: Explore nearby temples, shrines, and historical sites.

Things to Do

  • Relax in the therapeutic hot springs.
  • Stroll through the charming streets and visit local shops.
  • Savor traditional Japanese cuisine featuring seasonal ingredients.
  • Discover the nearby Rokko Mountain Range for hiking and stunning views.
