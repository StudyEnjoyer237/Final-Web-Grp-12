Welcome to Arima Onsen

Discover one of Japan's oldest and most renowned hot spring towns.

Location

To get to Arima Onsen from Kyoto, take the JR Tokaido-Sanyo Line to Sannomiya Station in Kobe. From there, transfer to the Kobe Electric Railway (Shintetsu Line) and get off at Arima Onsen Station. The total journey takes about 1.5 to 2 hours. You can also take a direct highway bus from Kyoto Station to Arima Onsen, which takes about 1 hour and 45 minutes..
